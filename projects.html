Annual Campaign September 30, 2025

LGMD Awareness Day

To mark Limb-Girdle Muscular Dystrophy (LGMD) Awareness Day, MDP organized a highly impactful public advocacy campaign. The initiative featured an **Awareness Walk** where community members, supporters, and patients marched together holding banners to raise local visibility for rare muscle diseases. Following the walk, an interactive **Educational Seminar** was hosted utilizing multimedia presentations to train doctors, caretakers, and individuals on diagnosis pathways, standard disease management, and standard rehabilitation tools.

Run for Rare Pakistan

"Run for Rare Pakistan" stands as a premier solidarity campaign where people from all walks of life come together to support rare disease warriors. The core objective of this initiative is to advocate for the fundamental healthcare rights and comprehensive social inclusion of individuals living with Muscular Dystrophy. By mobilizing communities, we actively champion structural accessibility and equal opportunities within society.

Pilot Project Sponsored by EveryLife Foundation 1 Mar - 31 May 2026

Provision of Modern Tools and Resources for Data Collection, Registration, and Support of Patients Affected by Muscular Dystrophy

Implemented by Muscular Dystrophy Pakistan (MDP) across Taluka Thul, District Jacobabad, Sindh, this crucial initiative establishes a community-based registration and identification system. Targeting 10 highly vulnerable Union Councils, the project bridges severe awareness gaps, distributes vital educational materials, and maps patient mobility and socio-economic needs to build a structured national registry for rare disease warriors.

Customized Wheelchair Distribution & Mobility Empowerment Program

To restore dignity, structural independence, and active community participation, Muscular Dystrophy Pakistan (MDP) manages a dedicated mobility equipment pipeline. We specialize in providing high-quality, customized wheelchairs engineered specifically for the unique physical requirements of children and young adults navigating Muscular Dystrophy, ensuring optimal ergonomic posture and long-term physical comfort.
